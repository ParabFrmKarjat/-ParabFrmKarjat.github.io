PARAB FARM WELCOMES YOU!!

Spread out in more than an acre, the Parab Farm is conveniently located some 80 km from Mumbai in the village of Kadav near Karjat. A very short drive from the Train Station at Bhivpuri will take you to Parab Farms. Alternatively, you can drive in your vehicle from Mumbai.

This is an ideal place where you can relax, rejuvenate, read, write, paint, go for idyllic walks, and basically unwind in the crisp air amidst nature.

Trailheads to popular treks like the famous Buddhist Caves of Kondana, Bhimashankar, and the Peth Fort are about half an hour’s drive from the farmhouse. Matheran is about 17kms from the place.

The Best time to visit would be during the monsoons from Mid -June to September and later during winter from November to January.

There is a Farm House which has two independent bedrooms a large living room with a fully functional kitchen besides two washrooms. The place is located in a beautiful well-maintained garden with a swimming pool. Rooms have comfortable beds, clean linen, and sunshine streaming in through the windows overlooking cashew orchards.

The bathroom is a modern one with a geyser attached. The rooms open out to a large verandah where you can sit and relax, else enjoy a dip in the pool.

A big group of sixteen can easily be accommodated in the farmhouse. We also serve home-cooked Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian food at a reasonable price.

You will have a cellular phone and data signal in case you want to stay connected.
